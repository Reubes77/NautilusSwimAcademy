Classes

Classes are from Monday to Saturday. Swimming lessons are presented throughout the year in a 20m heated (28°-34°) swimming pool. We encourage swimmers to arrive 10 minutes before the session starts as to allow for prompt start times to be met. The school is closed during public and school holidays.

Online registrations will be contacted within 24 hours of enrollment to confirm enrollment. If you do not confirm enrollment, then the registration will be cancelled. Online registrations can be made up to 30 days in advanced.

Fees

  • Beginner
  • In the beginners' classes, you will learn the importance of swimming and safety around the pool after which you'll learning more swimming skills and also swimming farther distances.

    We also introduce the swimmers practice doing jump-ins and floating on their back when they return to the surface.
  • R350 p/m
  • Intermediate
  • This class swimmers will refine their freestyle stroke and bilateral breathing and also be introduced to flip turns.

    The goal is to swim 80ft freestyle with bilateral breathing, and swim 20ft using the elementary backstroke. The ratio for this 30 minute class is 4:1.
  • R550 p/m
  • Advanced
  • This class focuses on increasing endurance and swim fitness. We introduce the advanced strokes which include both the breaststroke, and butterfly.

    We will also go over flip turns, standing/racing dives, and increase your tread time. The ratio is 4:1 and it is a 30-minute class.
  • R750 p/m
